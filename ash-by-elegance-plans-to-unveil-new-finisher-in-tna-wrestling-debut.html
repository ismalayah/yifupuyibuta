Ash by Elegance Plans to Unveil New Finisher in TNA Wrestling Debut

Publish date: 2024-07-10

A former WWE star, Dana Brooke, who now goes by the name Ash By Elegance in TNA Wrestling, seems to be preparing to debut a new finisher when she steps into the ring in her new promotion.

Ash By Elegance made her presence known at TNA’s Hard to Kill pay-per-view, where she was seated in attendance in Las Vegas. She closely watched the Knockout’s World Championship match between Trinity and Jordynne Grace, which Grace won.

Although the exact date of Ash’s in-ring debut for TNA is unclear, it appears that she has been diligently working on her wrestling skills in preparation.

Ash was recently seen training at the Coastal Championship Wrestling training facility, where she was photographed alongside former WWE star Gangrel.

In response to tweet expressing gratitude for her visit to the facility, Ash hinted at the possibility of incorporating some new and exciting finishing moves into her wrestling repertoire in TNA. Ash stated:

“Omg thank you for having me again and teaching me some new fun exciting finishers!! I’ll be back soon.”

At Hard to Kill, Ash was not the only former WWE star who made debut in TNA, as multi-time former world champion Dolph Ziggler (Nic Nemeth) appeared to confront the new TNA World Champion, Moose, at the end of the show.
