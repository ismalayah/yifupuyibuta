Iman Marshall

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Iman Marshall is an American footballer who is very young and promising. Drafted by the Baltimore Ravens, this young man has set himself to be one of the best players in the league. After all, that’s what all footballers want to be- best in the league.

Marshall played college football at USC  and if you know about football, I don’t have to remind you how important college years in football can be. He played wonderfully in High School and even better in college.

His game has improved after being drafted but he was already passionate from the very start. To be competing in one of the toughest games in the world is seriously not a joke. And Marshall isn’t joking either. he means business and he looks like he’s here to stay.
