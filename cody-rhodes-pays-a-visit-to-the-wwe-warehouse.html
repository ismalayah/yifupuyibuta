Cody Rhodes Pays A Visit To The WWE Warehouse

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Cody Rhodes has been out of action in WWE ever since he tore his pec and competed in the Hell in a Cell match back in June. Now during his recovery, he made a trip to his employer’s archives facilities and encounter a familiar prop.

On his Instagram Stories, Rhodes had a staredown with Triple H’s iconic King of King’s throne that he used for a couple of Wrestlemania’s in the past. That’s bringing back memories for Cody.

Rhodes has a history with Triple H’s throne. At Double or Nothing in 2019, he destroyed a replica of the throne prior to his match with his wife Brandi by his side. At the time, that was one of the more talked about moments from that pay-per-view.

It’s currently unknown why Cody was there. Maybe he had a virtual meeting with The Game and other executives regarding his future.

Rhodes is still recovering from his injury and there are rumors of him returning in the near future. We’ll see what happens next for The American Nightmare in WWE.
