Beyonc Looks Fashionable in a Colorful Ensemble in N.Y.C.

Publish date: 2024-05-24

It's been a week since Beyoncé took home the Fashion Icon Award at the CFDA's. On Tuesday in New York, she reaffirmed her style-icon status when she stepped out in a colorfully chic look. While leaving TriBeCa's Greenwich Hotel after a getaway stay with her rapper hubby Jay Z, the songstress was seen wearing a bold mix of prints in the most stylish way.

In the photo (below), the Lemonade artist rocked a striped midi pencil skirt with a floral kimono. To accessorize, she slipped into sky-high, burgundy platforms and held tight to an orange and black patterned handbag. In keeping with her chic mix of accessories, the always-gorgeous Beyoncé went with a pair of totally affordable (only $65!) round Perverse sunnies and a bold red lip to finish off the look.

While our girl Bey could have easily pulled off the classic black-on-black that's so commonly worn in N.Y.C., we appreciate her switching it up with a stylish mix of colorful prints for summer.
